Under the Hood

The Model Architectures

GANs

Image-to-image translation for urban layout infill and block morphology reconstruction. Balances adversarial loss with pixel-wise fidelity.

VAEs / CVAEs

Encode spatial data into probabilistic latent space. Enable diverse design exploration while quantifying uncertainty via ELBO optimization.

Diffusion Models

Multi-stage designs conditioned on spatial constraints and text prompts. ControlNet enables iterative refinement with persona-based feedback.

Parametric urbanism: the city as tunable variables — building placement optimized for natural light, cooling, and drainage simultaneously.

Challenges

What Has to Be Solved

The "Black Box" Problem

If an algorithm rezones a neighborhood, residents need to understand why. Opaque models erode the public trust that democratic planning depends on.

Algorithmic Bias

Models trained on historical disinvestment data perpetuate inequality — accelerating gentrification and displacing low-income communities.

Data Fragmentation

~28% of UK government systems are legacy. AI can't deliver on fragmented, incompatible, or outdated data foundations.

Environmental Paradox

AI data centers could need 123 GW by 2035. The infrastructure powering AI must not undermine the sustainability goals it serves.

What's Next

Agentic AI: Systems That Act Autonomously

Current AI advises planners. Agentic AI will reason, plan, and coordinate across domains in real time.

Task Planner Agent

Integrates flood maps, zoning rules, and demographics into a coherent problem map.

Sustainability Agent

Optimizes for net-zero energy and biodiversity in every design iteration.

Participatory Agent

Natural-language interaction with residents, translated into spatial configurations.

Projected market: $38.2B by 2030. These agents orchestrate as multi-agent systems — solving problems too complex for any single model.

2030 Vision

The Self-Managing City

Adaptive Smart Grids

Deep reinforcement learning balances loads automatically. Buildings trade energy via blockchain as "prosumers."

Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X)

Traffic lights communicate directly with autonomous fleets. Edge computing eliminates congestion at the intersection.

Dynamic Zoning

Street space repurposed in real time — parking becomes park space when demand drops, guided by live mobility twins.

Self-Optimizing Buildings

ML-driven HVAC uses occupancy prediction to zone heating and cooling room by room.

The shift: from master plans updated once a decade to living models that evolve continuously as new data arrives.
